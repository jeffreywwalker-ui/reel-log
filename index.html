Still On The Air

Jeff Walker — Reel Log

These aren't remastered highlights or a sizzle reel. This is the historical record — broadcast commercials like Coca-Cola's original "Holidays Are Coming," an Emmy-winning television series, and some of the earliest viral social content built around Nintendo, GameStop, and Halo. Much of it was found, archived, and re-uploaded by fans over the years, which is why some of the footage looks rough. But the comments and the view counts tell their own story — people still find this work, still watch it, still remember it. That was the exercise: finding the historical work that still resonates.

One heads up: if you're on a VPN, YouTube may prompt a sign-in before playback — that's a YouTube quirk, not a broken link.
